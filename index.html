Musartha

Our pre-workout pouch delivers a powerful, compact energy boost for any adventure or workout. Forget bulky energy drinks, perfect for when space is tight. Consume it directly and no one will know you are doing so. Instant energy, discrete, zero hassle.

Conquer Steep Mountains

The Problem: Hiking steep trails means every ounce counts. Bulky energy drinks take up precious backpack space and are heavy to carry.

The Solution: Our ultra-compact pouch fits in any pocket. Take it as is: no water, no mixing, no mess. Get your energy boost instantly, even on the toughest ascents, without weighing you down.

Power Through Every Workout

Designed for sustained performance. The blend of caffeine, beta alanine, guarana, and more provides clean, long-lasting energy that keeps you strong and focused from warm-up to the final rep: no caffeine crash and a consistent power throughout your entire session.

Need Energy On The Go?

The Problem: Busy schedules and constant movement mean you can't always stop for a meal or traditional energy drink. You need something discreet that fits seamlessly into any situation.

The Solution: Musartha is designed to be taken anywhere, anytime. Slip it into your pocket, bag, or desk drawer. No one will notice, no one will question. Get your energy boost instantly without drawing attention, whether you're at work, traveling, or in any social setting.

Key Ingredients

Caffeine

A natural stimulant that increases alertness and reduces fatigue, helping you power through intense activities.

Beta Alanine

An amino acid that buffers acid in muscles, improving endurance and delaying muscle fatigue during workouts.

L-Carnitine

Supports fat metabolism and energy production, aiding in better performance and recovery.

Guarana

A plant extract rich in caffeine, providing sustained energy release and enhanced focus.

Taurine

An amino acid that supports cardiovascular function and helps maintain electrolyte balance for optimal hydration.

Flavors

Natural and artificial flavors to make your pre-workout experience enjoyable and refreshing.
